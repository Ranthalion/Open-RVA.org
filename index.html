Open RVA Richmond's Technology Unconference

Open RVA is an "unconference" ,also called an "Open Space" conference, which is organized on the fly by attendees, for attendees. Open RVA is participant-driven, with topics selected by attendees on Friday night. A schedule is built, again by anyone who wants to participate in that activity, and then on Saturday the main event happens.

Open RVA is for anyone interested in technology, learning about technology, working in the tech industry, starting a tech company, or just being a part of the Richmond technology community.

Inspired by beCamp

Open RVA is inspired by the annual beCamp conference in Charlottesville, VA and is modeled after that event. Friday night is for introductions (with food!), topic pitches, and planning a schedule. Saturday is the main event, where we execute the schedule we planned on Friday night. It starts with breakfast at 9:00 AM and then sessions beginning at 10:00 AM. Sessions are 40 minutes long with a 5 minute break in between..
